Game Features

🤖 AI-Powered Villain

Face Claude, an intelligent AI antagonist that learns your movements and adapts its hunting strategy in real-time.

🔑 Puzzle Solving

Collect keys, solve environmental puzzles, and unlock the exit while evading Claude's relentless pursuit.

👥 Multiplayer Action

Play solo for maximum tension or team up with friends in cooperative or asymmetric multiplayer modes.

🎮 Mobile Optimized

Touch-friendly controls with joystick movement, look zones, and action buttons for seamless mobile gameplay.

🏠 Granny-Style Horror

Immerse yourself in dark, confined house interiors with realistic lighting, shadows, and oppressive atmosphere.

⏱️ Intense Survival

Race against time with difficulty-based timers. Easy (4 min), Normal (3 min), Hard (2 min) - can you escape?

Gameplay Modes

🎯 Solo Escape

Face Claude alone. Maximum fear, maximum reward. Prove you can survive the ultimate horror experience.

👫 Co-op Escape

Team up with friends to survive together. Coordinate your movements and share the burden of evasion.

🔄 Asymmetric Mode

One player controls Claude while others try to escape. Switch roles and experience both sides of the hunt.
